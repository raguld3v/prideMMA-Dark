Our Story

Founded in 2010 by former champion Mark 'The Lion' Riley, Pride MMA and Fitness Club started as a small, dedicated space for serious fighters. It has since grown into a premier facility, evolving with a mission to make the discipline and benefits of martial arts accessible to everyone, from beginners to professional athletes.

Our Mission & Values

Our purpose is to empower individuals through martial arts and fitness, fostering a community built on respect, integrity, and perseverance.
